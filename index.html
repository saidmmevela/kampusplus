    Lecturers | Professors

    You can now schedule appointments, schedule online classes, send notes, send assignments, collect assignments, collaborate with other teachers on supervising a class, get access to every student at a push of a button and more on Kampus+.




    University Students

    The Management

    Why spend money on printing while you have Kampus+? Now the management & student government can send announcements & information to thousands of students instantly on a single click via Kampus+.

    Why kampus Plus

    We believe the right information at the right time to the right person can change a person & the community around.

    Save Costs

    As the University Management will be communicating with the students through the application for free, the costs incurred in printing and distributing printed posters around different campuses will be heavily saved. In turn, the money saved can be used for other more useful purposes

    Convenience

    As the Professors and Lecturers will now be directly connected to their students. It will be so simple sending out information like notes, announcements also sending and receiving assignments. The teacher will send instant announcements like class cancellation as easy as putting a WhatsApp status.

    Connected Community
